Iraq and Syria wars

Some statistics and data visualisation of deaths and refugees from 2003 to 2019

Presentation of the content :


In this website you will find interactive visualisations of Syrian and Iraq war : Refugees and deaths from 2011 to 2019 You will find the 4 different visualisations below:
  • - Cartography of syrian deaths : Distribution of deaths by age, gender , actor and death cause for different Syrian regions
  • - Evolution of syrian deaths:Evolution of syrian deaths in time from 2011 to 2019 with details (gender , names, age, and date of death)
  • - Evolution of Iraq deaths : Evolution of Iraq deaths in time from 2003 to 2017
  • - Iraqian and Syrian refugees : Interactive treemap with plots of Iraqian and Syrian refugees from 2003 to 2019 :
    • - Demography of refugees by continent and country distributed by gender and age
    • - Departures of refugees to the diffrenet continents and countris (submitted or not)
    • - Number of submissions in different continents and countries

Datasets used :


  • - Violations Documentation Center in Syria (VDC) dataset list of syrian deaths
  • - Iraq Body Count (IBC) dataset Evolution of Iraq deaths
  • - The UN Refugees Agency (UNHCR) dataset number of Syrian and Iraq refugees in each country
